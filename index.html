ROBLOX GAMES

This section highlights the games I personally enjoy, The games in this archive have been chosen because they offer something special for both seasoned Roblox players and newcomers alike.

Here, you can explore a variety of genres, from action-packed adventures to creative simulations, and discover the ingenuity behind some of the most popular and innovative games on the Roblox platform.

Whether you're looking for new experiences or just curious about what's out there, this list has something for everyone. Dive in and see what makes these games stand out!

3008

Created on Jan. 20, 2019 | Made by uglyburger0

All Ages Blood (Unrealistic/Light), Violence (Mild/Occasional)

3008 provides a thrilling experience set within the vast aisles of an endless furniture store. As you navigate the unsettling landscape of home hardware in 3008, you're drawn into a realm of horror and survival. Build your base with items from the shelves and avoid the persistent chase of mysterious employees. The game draws inspiration from the Endless Ikea Backrooms concept.

Playing with friends is highly recommended to enhance the fun and excitement.

BOARD GAME INSANITY

Created on Mar. 9, 2021 | Made by valtkins | 2GO

All Ages Violence (Mild/Occasional)

Board Game Insanity brings a fresh twist to the classic board game genre. With its unpredictable gameplay, players are constantly engaged, racing against friends in a dynamic and exciting environment. The game features a variety of activities, from item collection and usage to intense minigame competitions and strategic shortcuts. Joining the 2GO group for special items like the Banana adds a unique incentive, making each game start with a delightful surprise.

I recommend having a party of 4 when playing this game, as it maximizes the fun and competitive spirit.

CATALOG AVATAR CREATOR

Created on Jul. 4, 2021 | Made by Muneeb | Catalog Avatar Creator

All Ages Suitable for everyone

Catalog Avatar Creator offers a wide array of avatar and catalog items to try on for free. With a vast selection that includes accessories, hats, limited items, hair combos, bundles, and animation packs, players can experiment and create their perfect avatar. Additionally, users can explore an extensive collection of over 15 million community-created outfits and save their custom avatars. Any purchases made in-game seamlessly transfer to the user's Roblox inventory, usable in all games.

I customize my avatars in this game when I'm alone or with my friends so I can have a matching aesthetic when playing with them.

DOODLE WORLD

Created on May 31, 2020 | Made by wish_z | Doodle World Studios

All Ages Suitable for everyone

Doodle World is an engaging and imaginative game set on a mysterious island teeming with unique creatures known as Doodles. Players can explore the island, collecting and trading over a thousand different Doodle skins, and engage in battles with other players using their Doodles. The game is updated every Saturday, ensuring fresh content and new experiences. Additionally, premium users enjoy the added benefit of receiving gems for every hour of play-time, enhancing the overall gaming experience.

I was obsessed with this game before starting college, I would recommend investing on finding misprints(shinies) as a mini trophy when playing this game.

EPIC MINIGAMES

Created on Jul. 29, 2015 | Made by TypicalType | Typical Games

All Ages Violence (Mild/Occasional)

Epic Minigames offers a thrilling assortment of 130 unique and captivating minigames, perfect for those looking to test their skills and have fun. As players win, they level up and earn coins, which can be spent on a variety of gear, pets, effects, and other exciting items to customize their characters. In addition to the main minigame mode, the game also features Epic Party, a lively board game where players can compete with friends or strangers to collect the most crystals, adding a competitive twist to the fun.

This game is great for enhancing your competitive skills on party games. Additionally, Epic Party is a preferred game mode when playing with your friends, it can contain up to 12 players and more.

FISHING SIMULATOR

Created on Feb. 18, 2019 | Made by CloudEntertainment | Cloud Entertainment

All Ages Suitable for everyone

Fishing Simulator offers an engaging experience where players can immerse themselves in various activities such as fishing, shark hunting, island exploration, and aquarium customization. The game continuously evolves with exciting updates, including new weather-changing items, a blacksmith forge in Nidhogg's Reach, and improved community server notifications. With free VIP servers and regular updates, Fishing Simulator ensures a captivating and dynamic adventure for all its players.

Personally, this game has a therapeutic gameplay as it doesn't have any time limits (unless it has a special weather during events). Either way, you can have an aquarium to display and showcase your best catches.

IDENTITY FRAUD

Created on Jan. 1, 2016 | Made by 32bitPC | Team M0THERB0ARD

All Ages Violence (Mild/Occasional)

Identity Fraud is an atmospheric and suspenseful game that immerses players into a chilling and mysterious world. With its haunting soundtrack and eerie ambiance, players are thrust into an environment filled with psychological twists and turns. It's a captivating experience that keeps players on the edge of their seats, making the game a must-play for fans of psychological thrillers and mystery games.

Before playing any other thriller games, you should start with Identity Fraud, it is a classic game and it would be much more fun to play with friends.

PENGUIN LIFE

Created on Mar. 25, 2022 | Made by PenguinUniverseRBX | Penguin Island Studios
